Lineage II New Player Guide
New Character Bonus
The first character on each server to reach level 6 is called a “new character“. Before a character reaches level 6, another character can be created on the same servera nd developed into a new character. A new character receives various benefits up to level 25. The benefits given to a new character are as follows:
Exdhange Equipment
Equipment can be exchanged in weapon, armor and accessory shops by paying the difference in price. All no-grade equipment can be exchanged in this way.
Assist Magic
The guides in every village will enchant new characters with assist magic matched to their level. New characters between levels 10 and 24 are enchanted with different assist magic depending on their level.
Additional Quest Bonuses
Additional bonuses are bestowed on new characters in various quests.
